Here is some general info about oranges: Oranges are a citrus fruit known for their vibrant color and refreshing taste. They are rich in vitamin C, fiber, and various antioxidants, making them a popular choice for boosting the immune system and promoting overall health. Oranges grow on evergreen trees and are typically harvested when they are fully ripe. They can be enjoyed fresh, juiced, or used in a variety of culinary dishes, including salads and desserts. Additionally, oranges are known for their hydrating properties, as they have a high water content.
